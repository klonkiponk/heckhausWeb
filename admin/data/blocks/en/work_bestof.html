Domicil Store / Store Design - Dusseldorf and Munich

The covers are off! Since the end of November 2013 both Domicil Stores (Dusseldorf and Munich) were opened to the customers with our new Store Concept! Splendid furniture and accessories obtained an attractive and exclusive space. On several store levels there are not only the settings of the different furniture collections, but also specific areas, such as the “atelier”, the “VIP room” or the “creative studio”. Here customers can ask for tailored advice and individually information.

Be inspired by the new Store Concept and enjoy the new Domicil world with all your senses!

Fitness First / Black Label Club am Marienplatz / Munich

The “Fitness First Club am Marienplatz” has been given a completely new and strong profile. Spread across over two floors of 1100 square meters, different new designed areas offer a wide variety of workout options. As a perfect addition to the exercises, the club provides a wellness world with sauna, steam room and a stylish relax area. In addition inspired by the comfort of an alpine lodge, the area offers a unique panorama of the Zugspitze! Corporate architecture meets heavy workout – and the marvelous view of the Munich Frauenkirche is complimentary available.
