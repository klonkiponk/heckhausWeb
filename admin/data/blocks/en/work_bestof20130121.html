Fitness First / Black Label Club am Marienplatz / Munich

The “Fitness First Club am Marienplatz” has been given a completely new and strong profile. Spread across over two floors of 1100 square meters, different new designed areas offer a wide variety of workout options. As a perfect addition to the exercises, the club provides a wellness world with sauna, steam room and a stylish relax area. In addition inspired by the comfort of an alpine lodge, the area offers a unique panorama of the Zugspitze! Corporate architecture meets heavy workout – and the marvelous view of the Munich Frauenkirche is complimentary available.

DEGUSSA GOLDHANDEL / WINDOW-DRESSING - MUNICH

The Degussa Goldhandel GmbH approached us with the exiting task to strengthen the external effect of its current 6 branches in Germany and Switzerland and to concisely communicate the Degussa product spectrum.
The design concept, presented by Heckhaus, currently runs through an intense development and realization process. As interim solution, illuminated displays using modern LED technology in combination with moving pictures, up-to-date bullion rates and at lightning speed refreshing print and campaign motives produce a bright appearance of these branches with a brilliant, long-range effect.
