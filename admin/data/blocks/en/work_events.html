EXHIBITION STAND – RIVA AND MILAN

Based on the upcoming shoe campaign of W.L. Gore we have implemented the new theme of “surround technology”. Ratiopharm Klinik /

MODULAR TRADE SYSTEM

Development and design of a modular, flexible and multi-use exhibition stand for a product launch of „Ratiopharm Klinik“. The special feature of our concept is the extremely variable use of the fair booth size according to the local situation. The central characteristics remain and the illuminated frame allows replacing the content at any time. Furthermore we developed a media tool which can be used as a desk and the surface in the stele can also be used as a presentation module for campaign motives, interviews or image spots.

Reebok /

Womensruns - Hamburg

With 3200 woman runners this years' Reebok Women's run in Hamburg was again a complete success. Long before the start the Women's Village was bustling with activity. The great atmosphere could be already sensed at the numerous booths of the sport goods manufacturers and exhibitors. Needless to say that everybody gazed at Reebok's booth that was designed by us. Here, the most up-to-date Reebok collection was presented to the woman runners in a striking way. After the successfully run in Hamburg the Reebok Women's run is following in Frankfurt, Cologne, Munich and Vienna.
