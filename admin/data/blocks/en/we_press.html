TEXTILWIRTSCHAFT / ISSUE 17 / SEPTEMBER 2012 - HEADS-CAREER

Heckhaus and two other agencies made it to the finals amongst countless applications of this year`s Window Dresser Award. From now on until the 5th of October, interested passers can vote for their favorite window - either directly in the Berlin Fashion Store F95 or online. In January, parallel to the Mercedes Fashion Week, the winner may design the window dressing of the Department Store “Galeries Lafayette” in Berlin. The aim of this competition is, to promote ambitious young designers in Visual Merchandising. heckhaus.de
